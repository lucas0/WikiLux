Alejandro Javier Garc a Padilla \( born August 3 , 1971 \) is the current Governor of Puerto Rico Prior to becoming governor , Garc a Padilla held various roles in the political landscape of Puerto Rico , first as Secretary of Consumer Affairs , then as member of the 24th Senate of Puerto Rico , and finally as President of the Popular Democratic Party At the national level Garc a Padilla is a member of the Democratic Party of the United States As governor , Garc a Padilla shares his legislative powers with the 25th Senate and 29th House of Representatives , which are both controlled by his
