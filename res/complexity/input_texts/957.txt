Stephen Gilfus is an American businessman and entrepreneur He is a founder of Blackboard Inc and CourseInfo LLC , where he held executive positions from 1997 to 2007 Stephen is known as a father of modern day eLearning , as the primary business , and technical lead of Blackboard Inc one of the world 's most widely used eLearning systems In July 2007 , Gilfus started a management consulting firm
