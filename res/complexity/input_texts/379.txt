Dave Syverson \( born June 29 , 1957 \) is a Republican member of the Illinois Senate representing the 35th district since 2013 Previously , he represented the 34th district from 1993 2013 Early life , education and career He was born June 29 , 1957 , in Chicago , Illinois , and moved to Rockford in his early childhood He attended Guilford High School and Rock Valley College as a business major Illinois Senate Syverson was first elected to the Illinois State Senate in 1992 He joined a group of newly elected conservative State Senators called the Fab Five As a member of the Fab Five , along
